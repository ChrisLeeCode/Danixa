Wireless Backhaul

f your organization resides in multiple locations connecting voice and data over the public domain can be expensive and in some cases limited. There are now a number of ways your business can stay connected without incurring annual costs associated with such services.

DANIXA will survey your locations using the latest software and hardware to determine the best solution to install or replace your existing products with from our portfolio. Options include point-to-point and point-to-multi-point variants depending on your organizations requirements.

We only use commercial grade products from manufacturers we partner with such as Motorola, Cambium, Proxim, BridgeWave and Terabeam. The technologies employed are either wireless or FSO (free space optics) or for resilience a mixture of both.

In most instances the deployment of a wireless solution reduces the necessity to have a true line of sight which offers greater benefits when used in metropolitan or geographically congested areas. Wireless offers link speeds of up to 1 GB per second full duplex with an average latency below 3 milliseconds as opposed to FSO which requires a direct line of sight. FSO throughputs are usually greater than wireless with link speeds of up to 10 GB per second and an average latency of around 1 millisecond. Both wireless and FSO provide higher levels of security making them even more secure than those used within the public domain. In most cases the ROI (return on investment) can be in as little as six months in relation to a lesser leased line service.

Biometric Security

In addition to the standard access control solutions we provide, we can enhance them with biometric technologies. Unlike conventional access controllers which use either manual input methods such as keypads or proximity methods of inputs such as RFID (radio frequency identity) cards, biometric devices use the unique features of an individual, such as fingerprints, retinal scans and facial recognition weigh-points to process algorithms which compare characteristics stored within a database.

Biometric readers can be used soley or in conjunction with conventional keypads or RFID readers as an additional layer of authentication. When a dual method of authentication is employed, levels of security are enhanced many times that of a single method of authentication. Our biometric solutions track both authenticated and non-authenticated attempts for access. Interrogation of the database will identify both authenticated and non-authenticated attempts for access displaying the captured image, timestamp and action taken to allow or restrict entry.

Uninterruptible Power Supplies

A crucial part to most businesses are their communication platforms and the devices that store the data. Safeguarding power supplies to these types of equipment can be done with the implementation of a UPS (uninterruptible power supply).

UPS’s have several functions, primarily to ensure the Power Supply to the equipment is clean and surge free from the national grid and also to allow a grace period for the equipment to continue running allowing shut down in a controlled manner following a mains power failure. Depending on your requirements Danixa can supply a standalone UPS capable of supporting a single machine through to a full size generator capable of supporting an office block.
