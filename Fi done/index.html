What is the MD Card?

 

Quite often multiple pregnancies are a shock and the budgeting for singleton pregnancy goes out of the window. Multiple families need support and help to give their babies the same opportunities a singleton would have received.   The MD Card is just that, an exclusive membership club for families of multiples giving access to discounts on days out, shopping, travel, clubs & activities and eating out benefitting the entire family.

Sign up and let the MD card help you make new memories and ensure your family days out are not a thing of the past.
