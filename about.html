Maintenance Packages

Our maintenance solutions ensure you receive the most comprehensive cover with a payment plan to suite your business.

Loyalty Cover

Offers duty bound contracts, for terms longer than a year to customers purchasing a DANIXA maintenance solution using an annual payment method.

Customers taking out an extended two or three year maintenance contract will have the peace of mind that there will be no price increase within the cover period provided it remains claim free.

Standard Cover

Offers a duty bound contract of one year to customers purchasing a DANIXA maintenance solution using an annual payment method.

Easy Cover

Offers a duty bound contract to customers purchasing a DANIXA maintenance solution offering flexible payment methods to companies who require short term contracts.

The overall premium is slightly more expensive than the Loyalty Cover, however it offers peace of mind that the contract can be terminated at any time provided it remains claim free.
