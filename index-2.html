About Us

Who are Danixa and what do we do?

  • Who Are We?

Danixa are a voice and data network provider offering a number of additional services to its clients such as CCTV and access control.

We offer a complete end-to-end service; from system design and installation, network services to support and maintenance. Our dedicated and fully qualified staff can provide independent, expert advice in any area of communications, access control or CCTV. We specialise in building the right solutions for small, medium and large organisations.

Anyone can sell you a business solution but with Danixa you will have peace of mind that we will provide you with the right solution tailored for your business needs and be with you every step of the way.

It doesn't stop there, Danixa prides itself with unparalleled customer service, support and are always more than happy to assist you with any questions or queries you may have prior to, during or after installation.
